Our Service

Why You Should Choose Us As The Family Holiday Option

At Feed My Sheep, we create joyful, hands-on farm adventures for families and animal lovers alike. Whether you're here to cuddle our friendly sheep, enjoy the fresh country air, or simply spend quality time together, our farm offers the perfect escape from busy city life. Come make memories that matter — just a short drive away.

  • Authentic Farm Life Experience Close to Home

    No long drive or hectic travel needed — just a short trip to immerse yourself in real countryside charm. You'll connect with friendly animals and unplug from the city in a peaceful, natural environment.

  • Wholesome Outdoor Fun for All Ages

    From toddlers to grandparents, everyone can enjoy something — whether it's cuddling baby animals, painting bright faces, or walking through our scenic paddocks. Our activities are designed to bring the whole family together in a safe, joyful setting.

  • Memorable Moments Made Simple

    We make it easy to plan a stress-free outing. With hands-on crafts, interactive feeding zones, and fresh open air, your family can laugh, learn, and create memories that last — without the crowds or complications of big theme parks.

ACTIVITIES

  • Face Painting

    Transform into your favorite character with our vibrant and creative face painting! Perfect for kids who love a splash of color and a whole lot of fun.

  • Feed and Pet Our Animal

    Get up close and personal with our adorable farm animals! Gently feed and cuddle with our friendly crew in a heartwarming, hands-on experience.

  • Craft Activities

    Unleash your creativity with our fun, hands-on craft stations! Great for kids and adults who love to make, create, and take home something special.

  • Back Paddock Walk

    Wander through peaceful paddocks on a scenic walk surrounded by nature and gentle animals. A relaxing adventure for the whole family to enjoy.
